# Partner with Equalify

Equalify is an Open Source project. We build accessibility tooling in the open, and we rely on institutions and contributors who share that work.

There are two ways to partner with us. They are not exclusive. Many partners do both.

## Opportunities

Institutions that pledge support receive invitations to Equalify's roadmap review meetings. Those meetings are where we decide what gets built next, and pledged partners get feature-selection priorities. If a feature matters to your institution, this is how to ensure it lands on the roadmap.

Partner institutions also receive greater access to Equalify core developers, so they can better host their own versions of Equalify products.

## Get started

Becoming an Equalify Partner starts with scheduling a call with the Equalify core team. Email <a href="mailto:b3b@uic.edu">b3b@uic.edu</a> for more information.

On the call, we will hear about the capabilities of your development team and discuss our specific needs. If your capabilities fit with our needs, we'll invite you to a roadmap call.
